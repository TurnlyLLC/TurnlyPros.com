Standard Turnover

Reliable, Professional Resets After Every Guest

Our Standard Turnover is the foundation of Turnly. It ensures your property is consistently cleaned, reset, and ready for the next check-in.

Included:

  • Full top-to-bottom cleaning
  • Kitchen and bathroom sanitization
  • Floor care throughout the property
  • Dusting of visible surfaces
  • Trash removal
  • Visual restock check (host-provided supplies)
  • Final walkthrough
  • Photo documentation after completion

Best for: Hands-on hosts who want dependable cleaning without surprises.

Standard + Linen Service

Fewer Headaches. Greater Consistency.

This tier removes laundry and bed preparation from your workload while maintaining the same high turnover standards.

Includes everything in Standard, plus:

  • Stripping used linens
  • Replacing beds with clean, prepped linens
  • Towel resets
  • Linen rotation handling
  • Consistent bed presentation across stays

Best for: Remote hosts, multi-unit owners, and anyone tired of managing laundry logistics.

Full Service Turnover Management

Truly Hands-Off Hosting

Full Service is Turnly’s most comprehensive offering — built for hosts who want their property completely managed between guests.

With Full Service, you don’t just get a clean rental. You get operational oversight.

Includes everything in Standard + Linen, plus:

  • Toiletry and consumable tracking
  • Restocking of approved supplies (toilet paper, paper towels, soap, etc.)
  • Inventory level monitoring
  • Basic inventory management (linens, consumables, essentials)
  • Turnover reports and condition notes
  • Proactive issue flagging when something needs attention

Your rental is cleaned, stocked, documented, and ready — without you lifting a finger.

Best for: Professional hosts, portfolio operators, and anyone who never wants to “run to the property” again.
